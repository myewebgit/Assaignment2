Stay Safe,
Stay Connected!!!

Stay safe, stay connected (2021) is a lightweight interaction zone created for Wikimedians in India. This effort is to ensure community solidarity where Wikimedians can meet and talk to each other. Around April 2021, India faced second wave of the COVID-19 pandemic. Many people and Wikimedians have been affected or have suffered a loss. While we were talking to some of the Wikimedians in India personally, we noticed that we are actually looking for a space to talk to each other. The lockdowns, and lack of personal meet and greet have made the pandemic even more difficult. This space can be used to meet and talk to other Wikimedians.
